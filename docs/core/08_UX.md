## ğŸ“± Mobile-First Design Principles

1. **Thumb Zone First** - Buttons reachable by thumb at screen bottom
2. **Offline Always** - App works without internet
3. **Minimal Clicks** - Invoicing in 5 steps, not 15
4. **Progress Visible** - User knows where they are in flow
5. **Undo Everything** - No destructive actions without confirmation
