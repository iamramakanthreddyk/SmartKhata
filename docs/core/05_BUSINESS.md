## üíµ Unit Economics

### Cost to Acquire (CAC)

**Channel 1: Organic**: ‚Çπ0 (SEO, content)
**Channel 2: Google Ads**: ‚Çπ200-500 per user
**Channel 3: Facebook Ads**: ‚Çπ100-200 per user
**Blended CAC**: ‚Çπ200

---

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Average Customer**:
- Sign up for FREE plan
- Convert to DIAMOND: 5% (avg 1 month)
- Lifetime in DIAMOND: 12 months
- Upgrade to PLATINUM: 2% (avg 6 months)

**LTV Calculation**:
- FREE: ‚Çπ0 revenue
- DIAMOND: ‚Çπ499 √ó 12 months √ó 5% = ‚Çπ300
- PLATINUM: ‚Çπ1,999 √ó 6 months √ó 2% = ‚Çπ240
- **Total LTV**: ‚Çπ540

**LTV:CAC Ratio**: 2.7:1 (Healthy is >3:1, aiming for this)

---

### Payback Period

**CAC**: ‚Çπ200
**Monthly ARPU**: ‚Çπ45 (average)
**Payback**: 200 √∑ 45 = 4.4 months
